Developer Openings and Requirements

We are looking for talented people to expand our team!

Mineplex is a fast-moving, demanding, and rewarding environment that gives you the freedom to work when and where you want and to meaningfully contribute to a successful and advanced project where there is always room for advancement.

We value initiative, reward excellence, and constantly encourage critical thinking and originality. Be part of a close-knit, cooperative team working to deliver premier gameplay experiences to the Minecraft community!

Want to apply?

Send us an e-mail to jobs-dev@mineplex.com with the job you are applying for, your resume and examples of your work.

Plugin Developer

Minimum Requirements:

- 2 years experience in Java.
- Experience with SQL databases.
- Experience with git source control.

Desired Skills:

- Experience with CraftBukkit/Spigot.
- Deep understanding of concurrent programming.
- Familiarity with test-driven development.
- Experience with Maven build management.
- Experience with Redis.

Desired Skills:
